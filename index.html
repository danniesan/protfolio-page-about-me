Weather App

This is my very own first Weather Application.
It is functional and simple to use, just type in any city and the weather for the day will come up along with the five day weather forecast for the week.

Along with the temperature, you will see the day and time (24hr clock), and you will also get details about the condition, humidity and wind-speed for the day.

World Clock App

One of my favorite applications to make, the World Clock Application
With the display showing you three different times from three different cities (Paris, Sydney, and Los Angeles). Under the city you get to see "Todays" date and to the right you see the time in big font (the hour, minutes and seconds).

You will be able to click on the dropdown box and choose from four selections: "My Current Location", Mexico City, Tokyo and London.

Scary story Generator App

If you love scary stories that are not too extreme, try out my Short Scary Story Generator.
All you have to do is enter a person (names: Daisy or fictional: Shrek, Coraline), place (hospital, school, basement), or a thing (tree, crib, etc). You can type in one word or two, even a small description.

This generator will create a short scary story in minutes that you will enjoy. You can use it as many times as you want and you will get a different story everytime.
